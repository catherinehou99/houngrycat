Ichiran Ramen 一蘭

09.21.19
Tonkotsu Ramen

Ending the trip at one of Japan's most famous ramen chains. For a while it blew up on social media because of its private single booths if you want to enjoy your ramen privately. If there is anything I'll miss about Japan it'll be the bidets and the ability to order food on vending machines. They only offer one broth (tonkotsu) hence the ichi in ichiran perhaps, but it was the best hakata ramen I've had in my life! Even though we had to peel the eggs ourselves at Ichiran, the marinade there penetrated all throughout the egg white into this warm brown color. The yolk had a soft and luscious texture that complemented the subtle traces of mirin in the egg white. It was not my favorite ramen, however, because we also have hakata in the states, so taste was very familiar. But after eating at Ichiran and going back to Shinsengumi (a ramen shop in 626), I found myself missing that deep savory bone broth from Ichiran.
